FAQs

1. How much do Clear Aligners Cost?

The Cost of Clear Aligners varies between Rs. 40,000 to Rs. 4.5 lacs (USD 540 to USD 6061) all over the world. The various factors which control the prices of clear aligners are type and complexity of case, treating doctor, city and the clear aligner brand chosen.

2. Are Clear Aligners effective?

Clear Aligners are the future of orthodontics. They are very effective in treating mild to moderate malocclusions and certain complex cases also by use of auxiliaries. Cases such as mild crowding, mild Spacing, teeth gaps, crossbite and proclination can be easily treated using clear aligners.

3. What is the Cost of Invisible Braces in India?

The cost of Invisible braces in India depends on various factors, such as the severity of the patient's dental problem, Clear Aligner Brand, city e.g. Delhi, Mumbai, Lucknow, Hyderabad, Kolkata, Pune, Kerala, Bangalore, Chennai, Chandigarh, Gujarat, Noida, Gurgaon, Jaipur etc. and the treating doctor. Clear or Invisible braces will roughly cost between 1- 4 lakhs in India.

4. How long do Clear Aligners Take?

Clear aligners or Invisible Braces as they call them can take anywhere from 6 months to 24 months to correct your problem.

5. What Foods can you not eat with Clear Aligners?

With clear aligners on you can only drink plain water but eating or drinking is not a limitation with aligners. You can remove your invisible braces, eat anything and everything that you like under the sun.

6. Are Clear Aligners Covered by Insurance?

Medical insurance does not cover Clear Aligners treatment but don’t worry there are a lot of clear aligner companies which provide you with the option of easy EMI OR Finance. You can start your invisible braces treatment for as low as Rs 4999/-* per month.

7. Do Clear Aligners Hurt?

Clear aligners or invisible braces don’t hurt as much as the fixed Metal or Ceramic Braces because the force they apply on your teeth to move them to the desired position is controlled. They may be slightly uncomfortable for the first few days but once you get used to wearing them, they are very comfortable and convenient.

8. Are Clear Aligners Better Than Braces?

Clear aligners are definitely better than metal braces in correcting mild to moderate orthodontic problems. The benefits of clear braces are many:-

  • They are invisible.

  • They are removable.

  • They apply controlled force over your teeth.

  • They help you maintain force over your dental hygiene properly.

  • There are no dietary restrictions with clear braces.

  • They do not cause ulcerations in your mouth like fixed braces.

9. Are Clear Aligners or Braces worth It?

Since clear aligners are removable and have controlled forces built in them, they do not cause any side effects or discomfort. The same is not true for fixed braces, which leave white lesions on your teeth after they are removed on the completion of your treatment. Fixed braces also cause root resorption which is negligible with clear aligners.

10. Do Invisible braces have any side effects?

Since clear aligners are removable and have controlled forces in built in them, they do not cause any side effects or discomfort. The same is not true for fixed braces, which leave white lesions on your teeth after they are removed on the completion of your treatment. Fixed braces also cause root resorption which is negligible with clear aligners.

11. Do I need to follow any guidelines after my clear aligners treatment is complete.

After completing your treatment your treating doctor will give you retainers which are very similar to clear aligners. They have to be worn for a few months in order to prevent relapse. Based on your orthodontic correction your doctor may also choose to give you fixed retainers which are also not visible.

12. What is the 6-month Clear braces cost in India?

6-month clear braces are also called clear aligners or invisible braces. They are affordable and invisible teeth braces cost in India starts from INR 40,000 and upwards.

Invisible braces are one of the most popular choices of braces in India for straightening teeth. With many international brands like 32 Watts Aligners, world-class invisible braces are now easily available in India. Teeth braces cost in India is affordable.

It is a myth that invisible braces are expensive. Indian brands like 32 Watts are providing global standards invisible braces at an affordable cost because they are made right here in India providing world class quality! They also offer easy EMI options starting from as low as INR 4999 per month!
